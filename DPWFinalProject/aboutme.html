DARLENE PÉREZ WIESNER...


My career path was not a direct one. For more than a decade, I built a career in healthcare collaborating with patients, families and other healthcare professionals, to assess neurological disorders, help people eat, speak and function in their day-to-day-lives again. While I did not know it at the time, this was the perfect place to hone the professional skills that I rely on and value today. These include being a good listener, creative problem-solver, excellent with time management, and having strong attention to detail. I needed all these skills with the reports I wrote on a daily basis.

It was when I was participating at the first Tech Moms in Color Cohort, TMiC, in the Fall of 2023, when I started to see a new path for myself -- a way to use my skills to identify and solve problems. During my time with TMiC, I received an introduction to the field of Quality Assurance and realized that my skills were inline with the skills required for this line of work.


Through the amazing community of women-tech professionals associated with TechMoms.org, I found my path forward and am making a major career change. I am currently making plans to take additional course work and acquire certifications to prepare for work in an entry level position as a Quality Assurance Engineer.
