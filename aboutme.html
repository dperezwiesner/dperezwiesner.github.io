My Story

My career path was not a direct one. For more than a decade, I built a career in healthcare collaborating with patients, families and other healthcare professionals, to assess neurological disorders, help people eat, speak and function in their day-to-day-lives again. While I did not know it at the time, this was the perfect place to hone the professional skills that I rely on and value today. These values include advocacy, facilitation and problem-solving.

It was when I was participating at the first Tech Moms in Color Cohort, TMiC, in the Fall of 2023, when I started to see a new path for myself -- a way to use my skills to identify and solve problems. During my time with TMiC, I received an introduction to ....

Through the amazing community of women-tech professionals associated with TechMoms.org, I found my path forward and am making a major career change. I am currently making plans to take additional course work to prepare for work in an entry level position with Quality Assurance. I plan to continue my study in preparation for work as a software engineer.
